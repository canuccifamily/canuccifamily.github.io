about us

Who are we?

We're the biggest and most powerful family in Free Penguin. We're known as the Canuccis.

When was the family started?

Well, on March 20th, 2017, a well known member (finneysin) got bored with the same old thing everyday. The next day, he decided to stat a mafia roleplay with a friend. It has now escalated to the point of having two families, and possibly more later on.

Join our family

Joining the family is as easy as coming up with a name, and creating a costume. We also ask that you keep your character's name on your playercard. Otherwise, you are free to do whatever.
